Are You Eligible?

CyberEdge Academy follows strict government-mandated selection criteria to ensure the highest quality of digital talent from the KP region.

Program Capacity75% Optimal

fact_check Eligibility Criteria

Intermediate Level Digital Skills

  • check_circle

    KP Domicile Holders: Exclusively for residents of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

  • check_circle

    Education: Bachelor's degree (14 Years minimum) in any discipline or currently enrolled in Final Year (Transcript required).

  • check_circle

    Background Quotas: 60% for ICT background, 40% for non-ICT but relevant field.

  • info

    In case quota for any category is not met, it will automatically shift to the other category.

Advanced Digital Skills

  • check_circle

    KP Domicile Holders: Open only to verified residents of KP.

  • check_circle

    Education: Bachelor's degree (14 Years minimum) in ICT or equivalent, or enrolled in Final Year (Transcript required).

  • check_circle

    Specialized Quotas: 80% for ICT background, 20% for non-ICT but relevant field.

  • info

    In case the 20% are not filled by non-ICT, the quota may shift to the ICT category.

Scrutiny & Selection Process

Our rigorous screening ensures only the most dedicated and qualified candidates join our ranks.

1

Regional Sorting

Applications sorted by District of Domicile to ensure regional representation.

2

Criteria Validation

Applications failing to meet eligibility criteria are discarded immediately.

3

Screening Test

MCQ-based online test with IQ, Basic Computing, and English sections. 45-minute timed.

4

Final Selection

Top performers per available seats selected for respective trainings.
