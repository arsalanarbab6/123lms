Is this program really free?

expand_more

Yes, it's a 100% government-funded initiative. There are no hidden charges, tuition fees, or registration costs for selected candidates.

What are the eligibility criteria?

expand_more

To be eligible, applicants must meet these requirements:

  • Must possess a valid Domicile of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (KP).
  • Age between 18 and 35 years.
  • Education varies by track — Intermediate requires FA/FSc/Bachelor's; Advanced requires Bachelor's in CS/IT or equivalent.
Full eligibility details →

How does the screening test work?

expand_more

The selection process includes a mandatory MCQ-based online screening test. It consists of sections on IQ, Basic Computing, and English. Once you start, you must finish within the allotted 45 minutes.

Can I apply for multiple courses?

expand_more

No. To ensure fair distribution, each student may apply for and enroll in only one course per session. Choose the track that best aligns with your career goals.

Will I get a certificate?

expand_more

Yes. All successful graduates receive a government-backed certificate. To qualify: maintain at least 80% attendance and score 80%+ in the final assessment/capstone project.
